**Sliding Door**

A functional sliding door that opens horizontally when activated. Can be paired with another sliding door to create a wide entrance. Great for compact or decorative builds.

**Desk lamp**

Similar to the Ceiling Lamp, the Desk Lamp can be toggled on and off with a right-click. Its compact size makes it an ideal lighting option for desks and other small spaces.
